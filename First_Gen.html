First Generation: Creating a Legacy

Am I a first-generation college student?

At the University of Michigan, undergraduates and graduate students are considered first-generation college students if neither parent nor guardian has completed a 4-year college or university degree.

Getting Started as a First Gen

First-Gen Gateway

The First Generation Student Gateway serves as a starting point to get connected to resources for first-generation students. Housed in the Office of Academic Multicultural Initiatives (OAMI), the Gateway is linked to several partner offices and is a home for support for first-generation students. The Gateway is open during regular business hours (8 AM-5 PM). You can use the space for:

  • Finding resources for first-generation students
  • Connecting to partner offices that support first-generation students
  • Meeting with First Generation Project Manager Terra Molengraff
  • Study space
  • Meeting space
